What We Study

Research Areas

Our work bridges fundamental two-phase flow physics and practical engineering challenges in nuclear and thermal systems.

💧

Pool & Flow Boiling

Nucleate, transition, and film boiling; critical heat flux (CHF) enhancement; surface wettability and micro/nanostructure effects on bubble dynamics.

❄️

Condensation Heat Transfer

Dropwise and filmwise condensation; vapor-liquid interface dynamics; superhydrophobic and hybrid-wettability surfaces for passive condensers.

⚛️

Nuclear Reactor Thermal Hydraulics

Departure from nucleate boiling (DNB) in PWRs; passive safety systems; in-vessel retention (IVR); accident thermal-hydraulics simulation.

🔬

Two-Phase Flow & Instability

Flow regime mapping; density-wave oscillations; void fraction measurement with X-ray/neutron tomography; subchannel analysis codes.

💻

Computational Modeling

CFD of boiling with phase-field and sharp-interface methods; mechanistic wall boiling models; machine-learning-assisted heat-transfer correlations.

🌡️

Advanced Cooling Technologies

Immersion cooling for data centers; two-phase loop heat pipes; spray cooling; thermoelectric-coupled phase-change systems.
